# SplitWatch-Support

**SplitWatch** is a multi-stopwatch timing app, built for coaches and officials who need to time multiple athletes simultaneously.

---

## How to use SplitWatch

1. Tap **+** to create a stopwatch for each athlete and name them
2. Press **Start All** when the race begins to start every timer at once
3. Tap the **Lap** button on each individual stopwatch as athletes complete a split
4. Press **Stop** on each individual stopwatch as athletes finish the race
5. Tap **Save**, give it a name (e.g. "1600m Heat 1"), and it will be stored in your history
6. Open the **History** tab at any time to review full split data for every athlete in every session

---

## Frequently asked questions

**Can I start and stop stopwatches individually?**
Yes. Every stopwatch has its own Start, Stop, Lap, and Reset button. The bottom bar controls all of them at once, but each one can also be managed independently.

**Is my data saved if I close the app?**
Yes. All session history is saved locally on your device.

**Can I rename a stopwatch after I create it?**
Yes. Tap the name on any stopwatch to edit it.

**Does SplitWatch require an internet connection?**
No. SplitWatch works entirely offline. No account or login is required.

**How do I delete a session from history?**
Press the 'x' in the top right of the session.
