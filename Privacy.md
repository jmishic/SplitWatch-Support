# Privacy Policy — SplitWatch

*Last updated: July 1, 2026*

---

## Overview

SplitWatch is a stopwatch timing app for iOS. This privacy policy explains how the app handles your data.

**The short version: SplitWatch does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Everything stays on your device.**

---

## Data collection

SplitWatch does not collect any data. Specifically:

- No personal information is collected
- No account or login is required
- No analytics or tracking software is used
- No crash reporting data is sent to any server
- No advertising networks are used
- No third-party SDKs that collect data are included

---

## Data storage

All data created in SplitWatch — including stopwatch sessions, athlete names, and split times — is stored **locally on your device only**. This data is never transmitted to any server or third party.

---

## Internet access

SplitWatch does not require an internet connection and does not make any network requests.

---

## Children's privacy

SplitWatch does not collect any data from any users, including children under the age of 13, and is compliant with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).
